Shadows

This is how we can use shadows. The shadow uses for parameters:

  • Horizontal offset - how much to the right the shadow is placed
  • Vertical offset - how much down the shadow is placed
  • Blur - how large is the area when the shadow fades out
  • Spread - how large is the area where the shadow has a solid "base" color
